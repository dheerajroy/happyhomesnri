Godrej Meadows

at Mahalunge Pune

Godrej Meadows is a premium new launch residential development that is situated in Mahalunge road, Pune. This luxury venture is sprawled across a land parcel of 7.5 acres and comprises of 1 BHK, 2 BHK and 3 BHK living spaces. Godrej Meadows ensures a convenient lifestyle and hassle free commute to residents by being placed close to Pune connecting various cities. This premium development is located in the most promising locales offers an excellent access to reputed educational institutions, corporate hubs, health care facilities, shopping malls and entertainment hubs. Mahalunge is considered as the most serene part of Pune with maximum green stretches in the region.
